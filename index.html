SKY SANCTUARY

Sky Sanctuary is an online environment in which high school students can earn all of the academic credits required to graduate. We will be utilizing this innovative game-like platform as a framework for education by partnering with pre-existing accredited online schools, to create a richer experience for students than they are currently able to provide.

SSP takes place in a massive, 3D multiplayer world where thousands of students can interact, socialize, and learn together. The world we will create for SSP is to be a place of immersion: not just a place of work, but a place to call your home.

Cancer Crusade

Cancer Crusade is a puzzle game for mobile devices that raises research money to find cures for cancer. The game takes place inside the human body, immersing the player in the fight against cancer by defeating cancerous cells through puzzle play. By fighting cancer in-game, players are contributing to the real life battle against the disease.

Consultancy

Hour Interactions has provided gamification consultancy services to groups like the American Red Cross with the goal of creating more efficient world bettering systems, often by harnessing people’s desire to be real world heroes through helping others.

About

Hour Interactions is a game design and game consultancy company with the goal of making the world a better place. Since our launch in 2013, we have worked on software and systems to improve online education, raise money for cancer research, improve information access in America’s poorest communities, increase blood donation rates, and more.
