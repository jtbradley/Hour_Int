SKY SANCTUARY

The Sky Sanctuary Project (SSP) is an online environment in which high school students can earn all of the academic credits required to graduate. We will be utilizing this innovative game-like platform as a framework for education by partnering with pre-existing accredited online schools, to create a richer experience for students than they are currently able to provide. SSP takes place in a massive, multiplayer world where thousands of students can interact, socialize, and learn together. It uses principles of design similar to those utilized to make games like World of Warcraft so entertaining. As a result, students are motivated to do their work because they are actively engaged while doing it.

By giving the right kind of user feedback, placing students in an environment of peer motivation, and rewarding them with in-game items, currency, and experience points for doing work, we will create an online school experience that feels meaningful and will motivate students to progress in their school work the same way games motivate us to keep playing for hours on end. Outside of lessons students will create avatars with which to explore an awe-inspiring 3D game world; a futuristic high school on an island floating in the sky inhabited by thousands. Here they will see and interact with the avatars of other students, spend currency earned from academic progress, and explore the virtual world’s many areas and activities. The world we will create for SSP is to be a place of immersion: not just a place of work, but a place to call your home.

Cancer Crusade

Cancer Crusade is a puzzle game for mobile devices that raises research money to find cures for cancer. The game takes place inside the human body, immersing the player in the fight against cancer by defeating cancerous cells through puzzle play. By fighting cancer in-game, players are contributing to the real life battle against the disease.

Cancer Crusade is more than just a money generator. Although this is a game for everyone, Cancer Crusade will be an especially empowering experience for cancer patients. Cancer patients can play the game and take a direct role in the fight against their disease, and, through an in-game social network, patients are enabled to connect with others. Cancer is not something anyone should have to face by themselves.
